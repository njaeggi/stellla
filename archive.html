Capsule Collection

In the first phase of my final year, we are allowed to design our own collection concept together as a graduating class of clothing designers. The process begins modestly with extensive research, followed by the planning and development of a concept, but unfolds its full potential during implementation and ends with the impressive final result - a unique class collection. Our theme “Capsual Wardrobe” serves as inspiration and starting point for the final collection. The entire process was documented in a brochure, from inspiration to the finished collection from my perspective.
